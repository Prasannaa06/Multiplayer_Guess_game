How to Play

  • Create or join a lobby with a 6-digit code.
  • Submit a number between 1 and 100 each round.
  • Target = 80% of the average submissions.
  • Exact guess = 100 points, closest = 50 points.
  • Lowest scores eliminated at intervals; last player wins!
